About Me

I'm a digital smile designer with 7 years of experience in CAD/CAM dentistry, specializing in Exocad. I create all types of restorations, including All-on-4, All-on-X, and full-mouth rehabilitations on monolithic zirconia.

I own a fully equipped dental laboratory in my hometown, which allows me to perform the entire production cycle myself ‚Äî from digital design to final finishing. This gives me a clear understanding of how each restoration should fit and function inside the patient's mouth.

I hold a degree in Dentistry, giving me insight into both the technical and clinical perspectives of each case.

Additionally, I've organized and taught courses for beginners on Exocad, as well as courses on staining zirconia with MiYO colors, sharing my experience as a lecturer with other dental professionals.

I work remotely, seven days a week, with turnaround times ranging from 10 minutes to 10 hours, depending on case complexity (delays are extremely rare).
